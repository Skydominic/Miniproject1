It takes time to become timeless

The latest version of Aventador is not only powerful, it is the most powerful Aventador ever made. With the highest-performing standard-production naturally aspirated V12 engine in Lamborghini history, it incorporates advanced technological solutions and unparalleled design. This gem, to be produced in only 350 units, is the final—and greatest—expression of a family of super sports cars that has left its mark on the last decade.




In the spirit of the first Aventador

The livery, developed specifically by Lamborghini Centro Stile, celebrates the peak of Aventador production with a harmonious balance of shapes, accentuated by a use of light and shade between the black and painted parts.


FEEL THE ENGINE

Let yourself be overwhelmed by the unmistakable sound of an engine that has made history and that, like a symphony for orchestra, reproduces sounds and emotions to say the least unique.


CENTRO STILE

The Aventador style combines pure lines and a sporting essence that magisterially blend together in the latest masterpiece of the range. Here, aesthetic perfection and unequaled performance come to life without compromise.


DRIVING DYNAMICS

In the new Aventador LP 780-4 Ultimae, the exceptional combination between the active steering system (Lamborghini Dynamic Steering) and the rear steering system (Lamborghini Rear-Wheel Steering) delivers dynamic performance that enhances agility at low speed while optimizing control and stability at high speed.
